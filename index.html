About Us


              St. Joseph's University, located in the vibrant city of Bengaluru, has a rich history dating back to its establishment in 1882 by the Paris Foreign Mission Fathers. Transitioning to the management of the Jesuit order in 1937, the university has continually evolved, achieving numerous milestones. Notably, in 1986, it became the first affiliated college in Karnataka to offer postgraduate programs, received a Research Centre in 1988, and gained academic autonomy in 2005. The journey culminated in the university's elevation to the status of India's first Public-Private-Partnership University on July 2, 2022, following the passing of the St Joseph's University Bill in the Karnataka Legislative Assembly. Inaugurated by the Honourable President of India, Smt. Droupadi Murmu, on September 27, 2022, this achievement marks a significant milestone for St. Joseph's University.

              Established in 2016, the Advanced Computing Department at St. Joseph's University has been at the forefront of cutting-edge education. The department initiated a 2-year postgraduate program in Big Data Analytics and has since expanded its offerings to include a 1-year postgraduate Diploma in Cyber Security in collaboration with HKCS Foundation and an Undergraduate program in BCA (Data Analytics). In 2021, the department introduced a 1-year postgraduate Diploma in Data Science, showcasing its commitment to staying abreast of industry demands. The MSc (Big Data Analytics) program, backed by the support and patronage of Tata Consultancy Services Ltd. (TCS), boasts a state-of-the-art laboratory established in collaboration with TCS. Professor Bimal Kumar Roy, a distinguished academic and former Director of the Indian Statistical Institute, serves as the guiding spirit and adviser for this program, further enhancing the department's academic excellence
